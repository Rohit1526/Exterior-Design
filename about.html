Who Are We?

Exteria & Co, a premier exterior design company, was founded in the year 2000 by visionary designer and entrepreneur, Jane Anderson. With a passion for transforming outdoor spaces into captivating works of art, Jane embarked on a journey to create a company that would redefine the aesthetics of exterior design.

In its early years, Exteria & Co focused on residential projects, bringing innovation and creativity to countless homes. As the company's reputation for excellence grew, so did its portfolio. The firm expanded to commercial projects, collaborating with architects and developers to enhance public spaces, corporate campuses, and resorts.

Over the past two decades, Exteria & Co has earned numerous accolades for its commitment to sustainability, incorporating eco-friendly practices into its designs. The company's dedication to superior craftsmanship, attention to detail, and client satisfaction has solidified its position as an industry leader in exterior design. With a talented team of designers, architects, and landscape artists, Exteria & Co continues to leave an indelible mark on outdoor environments, making them both functional and visually stunning.
